The FOC matrix $A$ — $k=2,\ h=3,\ \ell=2$

The two first-order conditions $\varphi_i W_i'=1$ are affine in the bid densities $g_i=G_i'(b)$. With rows ordered (L-FOC, H-FOC) and unknowns ordered $(g_L,g_H)$:

$$A\begin{pmatrix}g_L\\[2pt] g_H\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}1/\varphi_L\\[2pt] 1/\varphi_H\end{pmatrix}, \qquad A=\begin{pmatrix}a_{11}&a_{12}\\[2pt] a_{21}&a_{22}\end{pmatrix}.$$

1. The win probabilities

An H-bidder faces $2$H$+2$L; an L-bidder faces $3$H$+1$L. With $k=2$, win $=$ at most one rival above:

$$W_L=G_H^{3}G_L+3(1-G_H)G_H^{2}G_L+G_H^{3}(1-G_L),$$ $$W_H=G_H^{2}G_L^{2}+2(1-G_H)G_HG_L^{2}+2(1-G_L)G_LG_H^{2}.$$

2. $A$ is the Jacobian $\partial(W_L,W_H)/\partial(G_L,G_H)$

By the chain rule $W_i'=(\partial W_i/\partial G_L)\,g_L+(\partial W_i/\partial G_H)\,g_H$, so the entries are exactly the partials:

$$A=\frac{\partial(W_L,W_H)}{\partial(G_L,G_H)} =\begin{pmatrix} \dfrac{\partial W_L}{\partial G_L} & \dfrac{\partial W_L}{\partial G_H}\\[10pt] \dfrac{\partial W_H}{\partial G_L} & \dfrac{\partial W_H}{\partial G_H} \end{pmatrix}.$$

3. The matrix, in full

Carrying out the four derivatives and expanding every entry:

$$A=\begin{pmatrix} \,3G_H^{2}-3G_H^{3} & \;\;3G_H^{2}+6G_HG_L-9G_H^{2}G_L\,\\[10pt] \,2G_H^{2}+4G_HG_L-6G_H^{2}G_L & \;\;4G_HG_L+2G_L^{2}-6G_HG_L^{2}\, \end{pmatrix}.$$

Entry by entry: $\;a_{11}=\partial W_L/\partial G_L=3G_H^{2}-3G_H^{3}$, $\;a_{12}=\partial W_L/\partial G_H=3G_H^{2}+6G_HG_L-9G_H^{2}G_L$, $\;a_{21}=\partial W_H/\partial G_L=2G_H^{2}+4G_HG_L-6G_H^{2}G_L$, $\;a_{22}=\partial W_H/\partial G_H=4G_HG_L+2G_L^{2}-6G_HG_L^{2}$. The diagonal is "overtake own group," the off-diagonal "overtake the other group"; note $a_{12}=\tfrac32\,a_{21}$.

4. Factored form

Each entry factors as (scalar)·(monomial)·(irreducible factor) over $\mathbb Q$ (Nemo/FLINT — the two quadratics do not split further):

$$A=\begin{pmatrix} \,3G_H^{2}(1-G_H) & 3G_H\,(G_H+2G_L-3G_HG_L)\,\\[8pt] \,2G_H\,(G_H+2G_L-3G_HG_L) & 2G_L\,(2G_H+G_L-3G_HG_L)\, \end{pmatrix}.$$

The two off-diagonal entries carry the same irreducible quadratic $G_H+2G_L-3G_HG_L$, so $a_{12}=\tfrac32\,a_{21}$. The first column and $a_{12}$ all carry a factor $G_H$; only $a_{22}$ carries $G_L$.
